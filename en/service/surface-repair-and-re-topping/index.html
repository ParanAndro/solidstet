REPAIR AND RE-TOPPING OF POLYURETHANE SURFACES

A professionally laid polyurethane surface is very durable and resistant to damage. However, like any material, it may wear or be damaged over time if the minimum requirements are not met. The most common causes of damage are excessive or improper use. The repair of minor damage and preventing further damage will help to avoid irreversible devastation of the surface, which then cannot be repaired and needs to be followed by complete replacement.

What is re-topping?

In the event that damage to the top wear layer is too great and extensive for a localized repair of the polyurethane surface, re-topping should be carried out. Re-topping is a process of restoring the top layer of a polyurethane sports surface by applying pressure spraying. We have extensive experience in the repair and renovation of sports facilities. We undertake the renovation of any surface, regardless of its condition and degree of devastation, restoring its original appearance and functionality.
