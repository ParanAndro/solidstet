MINERAL AND RESIN SURFACES

Mineral-resin surfaces are seamless surfaces based on composites of natural quartz aggregate and polyurethane resin. It is a more ecological and human-friendly alternative to concreting, paving or asphalting. Mineral-resin surfaces are an innovative and ecological solution for transport routes, squares, and spaces around buildings – both public and private.

Advantages of mineral-resin surfaces

Mineral-resin surfaces are water-permeable:

  • substrates with a mineral-resin surface do not require construction of additional drainage systems, which significantly simplifies the design and implementation of the investment,
  • rainwater and snowmelt on mineral-resin substrates very quickly flow into the ground, therefore no puddles are formed and rainwater does not burden the water drainage system,
  • the water-permeability of mineral-resin surfaces allows a free flow of air and water to root systems, and thus a natural irrigation of plants neighboring the surface.

Mineral-resin surfaces are durable:

  • mineral-resin surfaces are resistant to mechanical damage - they can be used for both pedestrian and light vehicle traffic,
  • they are great in winter conditions - mineral-resin surfaces are resistant to frost and road salt used for deicing,
  • because they are resistant to UV radiation, mineral-resin surfaces keep their perfect appearance for a long time,
  • mineral-resin surfaces are resistant to mechanical cleaning.

Mineral-resin surfaces are safe:

  • due to their porous structure, mineral-resin surfaces are non-slippery, they are also safer during glaze,
  • mineral-resin surfaces are a semi-hard surface – they are relatively flexible, therefore they reduce the possibility of serious injury when falling,
  • mineral-resin surfaces are safe and friendly for all pedestrians and road users: high heel shoes, various types of prams, cyclists, rollerblades, etc.,
  • mineral-resin surfaces are stable and non-toxic.

Mineral-resin surfaces are aesthetic and easy to maintain:

  • the mineral-resin surface has a natural look, therefore it harmonizes with other elements of landscape and architecture - also historic buildings,
  • thanks to the innovative technology allowing for precise application of the composite and the available color palette, you can design a remarkable decorative color composition for the mineral-resin surface,
  • mineral-resin surfaces do not require expensive maintenance, are easy to clean, and look attractive all year round,
  • thanks to the durable and uniform surface, mineral-resin surfaces are resistant to grass and weeds.
Application of mineral-resin surfaces
  • all kinds of pedestrian paths: alleys, promenades, boulevards, walkways,
  • organization of urban spaces: squares, yards, atria,
  • bicycle paths, also for rollerblades, roller skates, skateboards, etc.,
  • recreation areas, active recreation spaces and playgrounds,
  • surroundings of buildings,
  • filling empty spaces around trees in parks, on squares, along streets, etc.,
  • parking lots and ramps for passenger cars.
