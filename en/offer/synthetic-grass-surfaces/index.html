SURFACES FROM SYNTHETIC GRASS

A traditional sports field is usually covered with a grass surface. Because of its properties, grass is an excellent and universal base on which many sports are played. The most important point when planning the investment is to determine the specific purpose of the pitch, then to calculate the costs of operating and servicing the chosen surface. On this basis, one of our available solutions will be an optimal choice.

Synthetic vs. natural grass

The basic choice is the between natural and synthetic grasses. When analyzing the needs and resources of most investors, synthetic grass surfaces have the following advantages:

  • the quality, comfort and safety of playing on artificial grass are similar to those on natural grass,
  • synthetic grass sports surfaces are extremely flexible, thanks to which the ball bounces really well and the feet receive safe cushioning - just like the whole body when it falls,
  • artificial grass is a durable and resistant, and can be intensely exploited without interruption, at any time of the year,
  • synthetic grass football pitches require significantly less maintenance and service than natural grass pitches, so the cost of maintaining the pitch is much lower,
  • synthetic grass surfaces retain their perfect appearance for a very long time, without need for special care,
  • synthetic grass can be installed and used both outdoors and indoors,
  • thanks to the fairly wide range of available colors, you can choose a synthetic grass color that suits the surroundings,
  • a synthetic grass surface is ready for use immediately after installation.
Intended use of synthetic grass surfaces

An important advantage of synthetic grass is the possibility of using it in a wide variety of sports, such as:

  • football,
  • volleyball,
  • basketball,
  • handball,
  • tennis,
  • badminton,
  • rugby,
  • field hockey.

The height and density of the synthetic grass are the basic parameters in an optimal selection of the surface for a specific sport. Grass can be divided into three main height categories:

  • football: 40-70 mm;
  • multifunctional: 17-25 mm;
  • basketball,
  • tennis: 11-17 mm;
Multifunctional synthetic grass pitches

One of the basic functional features of artificial grass surfaces is its universality, which is why it works well in public facilities (schools, public pitches, etc.). A synthetic grass surface can simultaneously serve athletes from many disciplines on a 'multifunctional field'.

A multifunctional synthetic grass pitch is created using multifunctional grass, made with monophilic or fibrillated fibers, 17-25 mm high. Across the surface, lines of different colors are painted for given disciplines: football and handball, volleyball, basketball, tennis, etc.
