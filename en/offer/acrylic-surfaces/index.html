SPORTS ACRYLIC SURFACES

Due to their anti-slip properties, acrylic surfaces are found in many places, e.g. on terraces, parking lots or in passages, but they are most often used in sport. Sport acrylic surfaces are one of the most popular materials used in the construction of tennis courts, multifunctional fields, skate parks and playgrounds. These surfaces have been tested by generations of professional athletes, and used to during official competitions at the highest levels for years.

Sports acrylic surfaces - properties and benefits:
  • excellent grip: when playing on an acrylic surface, the player's footwear is stable and holds well to the ground, which ensures adequate comfort and dynamics of the game;
  • very high strength and resistance: these types of surfaces are resistant to abrasion, wear and weather conditions;
  • easy and cheap to maintain: resistant to dirt and damage, requiring virtually no maintenance or service;
